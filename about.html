Our Beginning

We are a dynamic small business rooted in Plymouth, driven by a passion for meeting and exceeding our customers' needs. Led by Mr. Habtu, a seasoned professional with a comprehensive background in mechanical and electrical engineering, our team has expanded its expertise to encompass the intricate realms of plumbing and heating, with a specialized focus on natural gas and LPG systems.

At our core, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of services tailored to the demands of modern households. From the installation and maintenance of domestic central heating systems to meticulous boiler services and installations, we ensure that every aspect of your heating needs is expertly addressed.

Moreover, we recognize the importance of compliance and safety in the realm of gas appliances. Thus, we proudly offer landlord gas certificates for a variety of equipment, including boilers, cookers, and gas fires, providing both landlords and tenants with peace of mind and assurance of compliance with regulatory standards.

In addition to our heating services, we extend our expertise to the realm of bathroom and shower installations and maintenance. Understanding the significance of these spaces within the home, we approach each project with a commitment to excellence, ensuring that your bathroom or shower is not only functional but also reflects your unique style and preferences.

Above all, we strive to deliver our services with a dedication to quality and efficiency, all while maintaining competitive prices.
